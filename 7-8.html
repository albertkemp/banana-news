Number and Financial Mathematics

In Years 7-8, we work with integers, fractions, decimals, and percentages. We solve problems involving percentages and money, including calculating percentage discounts and profits.

Working with Integers

Integers include all positive and negative whole numbers, including zero. We learn to perform operations with integers and understand their real-world applications.
