Place Value to 10,000

In Years 3-4, we extend our understanding of place value to numbers up to 10,000. We learn to recognise, represent, and order numbers, and apply place value to partition, rearrange, and regroup numbers.

Data Representation

We learn to conduct chance experiments, identify possible outcomes, and describe events using language like 'certain', 'likely', 'unlikely', and 'impossible'. We also create and interpret column graphs and picture graphs.
